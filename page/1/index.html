Founder of Prmm

Prmm (pronounced 'premium') helps reinsurance companies (brokers and reinsruers) more quickly, easily, and economically experience the benefits of Salesforce. Learn more

Founder of SureUp

Helped consumers make better insurance decisions with SureUp, an insurance robo-advisor. The app gathered home and auto quotes from competing insurers and scored each quote based on how it fit each user's unique wants and needs. It helped users move beyond simple price-based comparisons by providing insights into the quality of the policies and the insurers behind those policies.

Reinsurance Broker at Aon Benfield

Helped insurance executives understand their company's risk and negotiate deals that moved hundreds of millions of dollars of insurance premium into the secondary market at Aon Benfield, the world's #1 reinsurance broker.
